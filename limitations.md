# Limitations

1. All data are synthetic and do not represent any real CDC program, jurisdiction, provider, or patient population.
2. Provider characteristics are simplified compared with real immunization program data.
3. Jurisdiction-level variation is simulated and should not be interpreted as real state performance.
4. The risk score is for portfolio demonstration and would require validation before operational use.
5. Logistic and survival analysis scripts are provided as analytic templates; findings depend on the synthetic data generation process.
